Jennie Crandall
Jennie is an undergraduate architecture student at the University of Wisconsin–Milwaukee, graduating in 2026 with a degree in Architecture and a minor in Music. Her design interests focus on the role of color in architecture and the experience of interior space.
At PALMYRA, she contributes to projects by creating digital renderings of spatial concepts using Adobe software and producing physical models that support the design process and help communicate ideas clearly to clients. Through her work at PALMYRA, she continues to expand her understanding of the architectural design process while developing her skills as an emerging architect.

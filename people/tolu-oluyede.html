Tolu Oluyede
Toluwalogo Oluyede is a fourth-year undergraduate architecture student at the University of Illinois Chicago, pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Architecture. She is passionate about design and the ways architecture shapes communities and everyday experiences.
This internship marks her first hands-on professional experience in architecture, and she is excited to continue learning, growing, and contributing meaningfully to the team. She looks forward to expanding her skills, gaining practical insight, and exploring how thoughtful design can create lasting impact.
Prior to this internship, Toluwalogo gained early exposure to the field by shadowing professionals at Design United Ltd. in her home country of Nigeria, where she developed a deeper appreciation for collaborative design processes and real-world project workflows.

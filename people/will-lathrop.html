Will Lathrop
Will Lathrop is a graduate student in architecture at the Rhode Island School of Design with a BS in architecture from the University of Illinois at Chicago. Although his background is in architecture, he finds inspiration in a broad range of design fields. In his academic and personal work, he has worked on projects in furniture, sculpture, landscape, and animation.
These interests inform his approach to architecture which seeks to create vibrant, inclusive spaces where design becomes approachable and comforting. He feels passionately that color is the crux of creating welcoming spaces in which architecture can begin to engage at a personal level.

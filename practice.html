PRACTICE
PALMYRA PLLC is an award-winning woman-owned full-service design studio that integrates architecture and interior design to deliver cohesive, thoughtful spaces.
The practice, like its founder, is named after the city of Palmyra in present-day Homs Governorate, Syria, a city with an independent streak and an incredibly rich multicultural history. In our practice we believe that we are never creating in a vacuum and the name of our practice serves as a reminder of that.
DESIGN APPROACH
At PALMYRA we believe that people respond to the environments they inhabit and are shaped by them. We believe that architecture is a deeply humanistic endeavor, one that requires empathy, conviction, and an insatiable appetite for knowledge.
We love to listen to the needs and desires of our clients and end users and help them realize their vision by infusing it with spatial thinking, holistic planning, and technical expertise. We delight in solving complex problems while also seeking the poetry in every moment we design.
In our work we strive to adopt this exacting but empathic approach to design.

FENIX FAMILY HEALTH
With this renovation and expansion, Fenix Family Health, a non-profit clinic dedicated to providing affordable and dignified healthcare to the Spanish-speaking communnities of Chicago's northern suburbs, will be able to expand its services to offer its clients prenatal and dental care while also hosting centering pregnancy courses and other health and wellness workshops in its versatile multipurpose spaces.
Type of Project: Interiors, Healthcare
Location: Highwood, IL
Status: Under Construction
